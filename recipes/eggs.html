Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 2 branches of cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice the onion
  2. Bring a pan to low-medium heat
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to pan
  4. Sauté onion until it is transluscent
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan with the sautéed onions
  6. Let the eggs cook until they begin to harden, then flip them
  7. Now shut off the heat and let it finish cooking for about a minute
  8. Enjoy with beans and quesadillas!
