Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat pan or comal to medium-high heat
  2. Heat tortillas until tortilla starts rising
  3. Flip tortilla then add cheese to one half of tortilla
  4. Fold quesadillas in half and heat up for half a minute
  5. Flip quesadilla over
  6. Remove and enjoy
